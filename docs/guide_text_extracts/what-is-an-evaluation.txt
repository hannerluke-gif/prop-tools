TITLE:
What is a Prop Firm Evaluation?

SUBTITLE:
An evaluation is a rules-based test in a demo account. Hit the profit target without breaking drawdown or time rules to pass.

MAIN CONTENT:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Pass checklist
 
 Define trade plan and max daily loss
 Know your drawdown math (trailing/static)
 Track adherence every session

STRUCTURED FAQ (Schema.org):
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Q: Typical rules?
A: Profit target, trailing or static drawdown, daily loss limits, and sometimes time or news restrictions.

Q: One-phase vs two-phase?
A: Two-phase splits the target across steps; one-phase is faster but may cost more. Choose what fits your style and budget.
