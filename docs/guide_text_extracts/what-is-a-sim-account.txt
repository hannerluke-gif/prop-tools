TITLE:
What is a Sim Account? (Practice Trading Without Risk)

SUBTITLE:
A simulated account lets you practice trading with virtual money before risking real capital.

MAIN CONTENT:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
What is a Sim Account?
- A simulated (sim) account mirrors live markets but uses virtual money.
- It allows traders to practice strategies without financial risk.

Why Use a Sim Account?
1. Build confidence without losing money.
2. Learn order entry, risk controls, and platform navigation.
3. Test new strategies in real-time market conditions.

FAQ SECTION:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Common questions

Do sim accounts feel like real trading?
Yes, the data is live, but emotions are different since no money is on the line.

Can I pass a prop firm evaluation using only a sim account?
No. Prop firms require you to trade on their evaluation accounts, which are simulated but tied to rules and funding pathways.
