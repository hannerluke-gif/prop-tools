TITLE:
What is a Sim Account?

SUBTITLE:
A simulated account mirrors live trading without real capital. It’s where you learn rules and test a strategy safely.

MAIN CONTENT:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Why sim matters
 
 Build execution discipline
 Practice risk rules without losses
 Collect stats to choose account size

STRUCTURED FAQ (Schema.org):
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Q: Are fills realistic?
A: They approximate market behavior but won’t match real slippage/liquidity perfectly. Use sim to validate rules and process first.

Q: How long should I stay in sim?
A: Until you can follow your plan and show stable results across market conditions—often weeks to months.
