TITLE:
Personal Account vs Prop Account — Which Should I Start With?

SUBTITLE:
Personal accounts offer full control and full risk. Prop accounts add rules and splits but reduce initial capital needs.

MAIN CONTENT:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Side-by-side
 
 Personal: Full control, no splits, higher capital & emotional risk.
 Prop: Lower entry cost, rules & splits, structured discipline.

STRUCTURED FAQ (Schema.org):
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Q: Which is cheaper to start?
A: Upfront, prop can be cheaper due to smaller fees than cash funding a brokerage account—though ongoing subscriptions and splits apply.

Q: Which is better for learning?
A: Many beginners learn discipline faster under prop rules, then transition or add a personal account later.
