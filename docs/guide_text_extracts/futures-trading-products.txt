STRUCTURED FAQ (Schema.org):
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Q: Can I trade crypto futures with prop firm capital?
A: Some prop firms allow CME crypto futures like Micro Bitcoin (MBT) and Micro Ether (MET), but availability varies. Always check with your specific firm.

Q: What are the most popular futures for beginners?
A: Most beginners start with equity index futures like ES/MES (S&P 500) or NQ/MNQ (Nasdaq) due to their liquidity and smaller contract sizes with micros.
