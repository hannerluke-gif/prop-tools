Common Futures Products

  • Equity index futures (ES, NQ, YM, RTY)
  • Micro equity index futures (MES, MNQ, M2K, MYM)
  • Commodity futures (CL, GC, NG, ZS)
  • Currency futures (6E, 6J, 6B)
  • Bonds and rates (ZN, ZB, GE)

Some prop firms allow CME crypto futures like Micro Bitcoin (MBT) and Micro Ether (MET), but availability varies. Always check with your specific firm before trading crypto products.

Most beginners start with equity index futures like ES/MES (S&P 500) or NQ/MNQ (Nasdaq) due to their liquidity and smaller contract sizes with micros.
